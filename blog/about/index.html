About me

Hi, welcome to my (maybe) personal, mathematical, computer science and other things blog.

As I am an imperfect real human, and not a spider, I make many mistakes of many kinds and usually I’m not able to detect them on time. So, please, don’t hesitate to write to me if you find any grammatical, typographical, computational, mathematical, logical, illogical, physical or science fictional mistake. I thank you with all my guts! But please don’t if you are a panda or a koala, because I don’t like how all humans treat you. It seems like they think you’re like gods and not like species that are doomed because are not resilient to change and eat only one type of vegetable. So go and make cute faces to others, not to me.
